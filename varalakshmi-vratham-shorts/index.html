Varalakshmi Alankaram Checklist 2026

Before pooja day, first decide what setup you are dressing.

Step 1

Decide your setup type

Full idol
Amman mugham
Kalasam
Altar display
Step 2

Check the visible jewellery

Face, crown or kireedam, haram, hastham, padam, earrings, vaddanam, and thomala or vagamalai.

Step 3

Decor depends on space

Banana trees, coconut stand, lotus asana, and arch should match your altar space and family tradition.

Owner tip

Do not leave sizing for the last week

Thomala and vagamalai mainly depend on shoulder width and drape space, not only idol height.
