# Dance Product Controlled Vocabulary

Use these values for `dance.*` product metafields. Keep values stable so filters, theme logic, SEO, and GEO/AEO data do not drift.

## Core

`dance_form_suitable`
- Bharatanatyam
- Kuchipudi

`dance_range`
- regular dance
- real kemp
- black kemp

`product_tier`
- regular
- premium
- professional stage

`performance_context`
- arangetram
- stage performance
- practice
- class
- ceremony
- school program
- dance institute program

`buyer_context`
- kids
- adult dancer
- dance institute
- teacher
- bridal classical styling

`measurement_confidence`
- Measured
- Owner confirmed
- Inferred from product photos
- Check product photos
- Not applicable

When `measurement_confidence` is `Not applicable`, do not show a measurement panel on the product page.
