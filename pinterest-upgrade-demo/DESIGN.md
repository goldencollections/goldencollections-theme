## Style Prompt

Create a calm, reviewer-facing SaaS workflow demo for Golden Collections. The visual language should feel trustworthy, operational, and premium: warm ivory backgrounds, charcoal text, muted gold brand accents, ruby status highlights, and clear product-dashboard structure. The video should be easy for a platform reviewer to follow in one pass.

## Colors

- Background: #fbf7ef
- Surface: #ffffff
- Text: #241f1c
- Muted text: #6f6257
- Border: #e5d8c7
- Gold accent: #b8872f
- Ruby action: #c9223d
- Success: #157347

## Typography

- Primary: Inter, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif
- Use strong but restrained dashboard sizing. Avoid oversized marketing headline treatment except the opening title.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic neon SaaS colors.
- Do not imply a Trial app has created a live production Pin.
- Do not hide the OAuth or board-selection steps.
- Do not make decorative visuals more prominent than the workflow.
