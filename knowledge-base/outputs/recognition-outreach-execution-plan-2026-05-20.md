# Recognition Outreach Execution Plan

Generated: 2026-05-20

## Strategic Decision

Run two outreach tracks in parallel:

1. Bharatanatyam/Kuchipudi recognition track.
2. Deity/Varalakshmi alankaram recognition track.

The dance track should lead because Bharatanatyam has the most stock and fastest movement. The deity track should run beside it because deity jewellery has the highest margin and Varalakshmi season is active from May through August.

## Next Step

Build the first outreach asset before contacting anyone:

`Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi Arangetram Jewellery Checklist`

This should be a useful public page or guide that editors, teachers, parents, and forums can link to. It should not feel like a product ad.

Why this first:

- Narthaki, Sruti, India Currents, Khabar, and dance schools need a useful story/resource to react to.
- It strengthens Golden Collections' own SEO before any external campaign.
- It gives Anil a clean link to share on WhatsApp, LinkedIn, email, and teacher outreach.
- It supports the highest-stock, fastest-moving category.

Deliverables:

- Public arangetram jewellery checklist page.
- One-page Golden Collections media kit.
- Simple teacher/school partner PDF or landing page.
- Outreach tracker spreadsheet.

Arangetram checklist should cover:

- What is included in a Bharatanatyam/Kuchipudi jewellery set.
- Regular range vs real kemp vs black kemp.
- Kids, teens, adults, arangetram, and institute buying.
- Stage visibility, comfort, matching, finish, weight, and teacher approval.
- Short necklace, long haram, nethi chutti, mattal, earrings, vanki, oddiyanam/vaddanam, rakodi, jada, sun/moon ornaments, ghungroo/salangai.
- Links to the relevant Golden Collections collections and guides.

Media kit should include:

- Safe product categories.
- Shipping regions.
- Links to Anil page, Knowledge Hub, real kemp guide, Varalakshmi examples, Hanuman proof story, and measurement guide.
- 3-5 approved images.
