## Purpose

Build permission-based proof stories for Varalakshmi Vratham, Varamahalakshmi Vratham, Lakshmi Pooja, and Amman alankaram using the same safe pattern as the Hanuman Jayanti temple proof asset:

- real customer, temple, or devotee setup
- written permission before use
- factual description of what Golden Collections supplied or helped with
- grateful tone
- no endorsement language unless the permission text explicitly allows it

The proof story should support trust for deity jewellery and Varalakshmi alankaram shoppers. It should not become a heavy sales page or a ritual-instruction article.

## Photo And Video Checklist

Ask for original files where possible, not compressed screenshots. Do not require perfect production quality; real proof matters more than studio polish.

### Minimum Useful Asset Set

- One full front view of the finished setup.
- One close-up of the face, crown, earrings, nose ring, and forehead area.
- One close-up of necklace/haram/vaddanam/hastham/padam placement.
- One wider altar or mandapam view if decor items are part of the story.
- One 10-30 second vertical video for Reels/Shorts/WhatsApp, if available.

### Best Website Hero

Use the clearest full-setup image where:

- the deity face is visible
- jewellery is visible without heavy blur
- the setup is respectful and complete
- there are no unapproved people in frame
- no private home details are exposed unless approved

### Detail Images

Prioritize details that teach fit:

- crown height and width against face or hair
- face/mugham visibility after jewellery placement
- haram drop against the body or saree drape
- vaddanam placement after dressing
- hastham/padam scale and position
- thomala or vagamalai shoulder width and drop
- altar depth, side clearance, arch clearance, lotus asana, coconut stand, or banana tree placement

### Video Checks

Before publishing a video, confirm:

- permission covers video, not only photo
- audio does not include private conversation
- no unapproved people or minors are visible
- no personal address, phone number, order details, or private documents are visible
- temple or community name appears only if approved
- the caption uses factual "used for" language, not endorsement language

### Asset File Naming

Use a consistent internal naming pattern:

- `varalakshmi-[city-or-private]-[setup-type]-full-[date].jpg`
- `varalakshmi-[city-or-private]-[setup-type]-detail-crown-[date].jpg`
- `varalakshmi-[city-or-private]-[setup-type]-detail-haram-[date].jpg`

If privacy is requested, use `private` instead of the person, temple, or city name.

## Safe Wording Guardrails

### Required Tone

- factual
- grateful
- specific
- culturally respectful
- useful to future buyers
- careful about privacy and permission

### Safe Phrases

- "shared with permission"
- "photos shared with permission"
- "Golden Collections items were used in this Varalakshmi alankaram"
- "Golden Collections deity jewellery was used for this Varalakshmi Vratham setup"
- "the setup shows how crown height, face visibility, haram drop, and altar space matter"
- "this example can help families compare measurements before ordering"
- "follow your family, priest, temple, or local panchang for ritual procedure and timing"

### Proof Sentence Template

Use a specific proof sentence once facts are confirmed:

"Golden Collections [item/category] was used for [occasion] alankaram in [approved location/setup wording], shared with permission by [approved name/privacy wording]."

Examples:

- "Golden Collections Varalakshmi deity jewellery was used for a Varalakshmi Vratham home alankaram in Hyderabad, shared with permission by the family."
- "Golden Collections Varalakshmi face, crown, hastham and padam were used for a Varamahalakshmi Habba setup in Bengaluru, shared with permission by the customer."
- "Golden Collections deity jewellery was used for a Lakshmi Amman community alankaram in [city], shared with permission by the organizer."

### Avoid Unless Written Permission Says Exactly This

- official supplier
- temple-approved
- priest-approved
- endorsed by the temple
- endorsed by the family
- certified by the temple
- preferred supplier
- recommended by the priest
- blessed by the temple
- guaranteed auspicious
- complete pooja kit
- everything required for Varalakshmi Vratham
- fits every idol
- best Varalakshmi jewellery
- authentic ritual requirement

### Claims To Keep Conservative

- Do not promise ritual completeness. Say product planning and alankaram guidance only.
- Do not imply the same jewellery fits every idol, doll, face, or kalasam setup.
- Do not claim a temple, priest, or family recommends Golden Collections unless the exact wording is approved in writing.
- Do not publish private home, temple, or event details beyond approved wording.
- Do not say a product includes a component unless the product/order record confirms it.
- Do not publish material, plating, stone, or delivery claims unless confirmed from product/order data or owner notes.

## Publishing Sequence

### 1. Build The Lead List

- Past Varalakshmi buyers from May-August.
- Customers who bought Varalakshmi faces, dolls, hastham/padam, crowns, harams, thomala/vagamalai, banana trees, lotus asana, coconut stands, or deity decor.
- Temple, priest, or community contacts with existing relationship and permission-safe communication.
- International customers who can show shipping and setup proof without exposing private details.

### 2. Send Permission-Safe WhatsApp Outreach

- Send only to opted-in or existing relationship contacts.
- Keep the first ask simple: photo/video plus permission.
- Do not ask for a "review" unless the goal is specifically a testimonial and permission is clear.

### 3. Capture Permission And Facts

- Save the permission text.
- Record approved name and location wording exactly.
- Record channels approved for use.
- Record whether temple/community names can appear.
- Record any privacy limits before downloading or renaming assets.

### 4. Collect And Sort Assets

- Pick one full setup image as the hero candidate.
- Pick 1-3 detail images that show fit or component placement.
- Pick one short vertical video only if permission and privacy checks pass.
- Rename files with privacy-safe internal names.

### 5. Draft The Case Study

- Use the proof sentence template.
- Keep the opening factual and specific.
- Explain what buyers can learn from the setup.
- Add only relevant links.
- Mark any unconfirmed item as `Needs confirmation` inside the draft, not public copy.

### 6. Owner And Permission Review

- Anil or the owner reviews factual accuracy.
- The person who gave permission reviews name/location/quote if needed.
- Remove any claim that sounds like endorsement unless written permission approves it.
- Confirm photos/videos are approved for the intended channels.

### 7. Publish Website Asset

Recommended first asset:

- Blog case study if the story has enough detail.
- Proof hub entry if the story is short.
- Internal proof block on relevant guide pages after the main asset is live.

No live change should happen until final approval.

### 8. Repurpose After Website Publish

- WhatsApp share copy with the final URL.
- Instagram Reel / YouTube Short caption if video is approved.
- Pinterest pin from the hero image or vertical crop.
- Internal link from Varalakshmi checklist/guide pages.
- Add the story to future proof-story hub or Knowledge Hub if appropriate.

### 9. Indexing And Tracking

After publishing:

- Request indexing in Google Search Console.
- Track final URL, approved assets, permission source, and linked collections.
- Review performance during Varalakshmi season.
- Reuse the strongest fit lessons in collection copy or guide pages only after approval.
