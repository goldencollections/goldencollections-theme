## Scope

This list is for a first-wave USA diaspora dance-school outreach campaign around the Golden Collections `Bharatanatyam Arangetram and Kuchipudi Rangapravesam Jewellery Checklist`.

The list uses public websites and official school/contact pages only. It does not include student contacts, parent contacts, private group posts, personal social profiles, scraped directories as primary sources, or private community data.

## Compliance Rules

1. Do not send outreach until the checklist page is live, verified, and owner-approved.
2. Use contact forms and public business/school emails first.
3. Phone numbers are marked manual-use only. Do not use them for bulk calling, cold SMS, WhatsApp blasts, autodialing, or automated follow-up.
4. No student, parent, or private group data should be collected or imported.
5. Every target row must include a source URL from an official school/contact page where possible.
6. First message must be educational and transparent. Do not imply endorsement, teacher approval, preferred supplier status, guaranteed delivery, or official partnership.
7. Include a simple opt-out line in any direct email, such as: `If this is not useful for your school, reply "no outreach" and we will not follow up.`
8. Keep USA shipping language careful: Golden Collections usually turns around USA orders within a week, but outreach must still say families should plan early and allow time for customs, local delivery, costume trial, teacher approval, and missing-component checks.

## Recommended Outreach Sequence

### Step 1 - Preflight

- Confirm exact live checklist URL: `https://www.goldencollections.com/pages/bharatanatyam-arangetram-kuchipudi-rangapravesam-jewellery-checklist`.
- Recheck the live checklist URL in media-kit templates.
- Confirm image set is approved.
- Confirm whether outreach stays education-led only or mentions bulk/institute support by request.

### Step 2 - Contact Forms And Emails

Use the school contact form or public business email. Send one concise message:

- Acknowledge the teacher/school.
- Share the checklist as a practical parent resource.
- State it supports the teacher's list and does not replace it.
- Mention Golden Collections usually turns around USA orders within a week, but avoid delivery guarantees.
- Offer to share a shorter teacher handout or review component lists by request.

### Step 3 - Follow-Up

If no reply:

- One follow-up after 7 to 10 days.
- No more than one follow-up unless the teacher replies.
- Mark `no outreach` or unsubscribe requests immediately.

### Step 4 - Tracking

Track:

- Date contacted.
- Channel used.
- Reply status.
- Follow-up date.
- Whether the teacher requested the checklist, images, bulk support, or component review.
- Referral traffic or WhatsApp inquiries only after the page is live.

## Channel Policy
