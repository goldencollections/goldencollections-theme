## Before You Choose Deity Jewellery

Measure the idol and the exact placement area before buying a crown, mukut, kireedam, short necklace, long haram, vaddanam, earrings, nose ring, tilak, namam, shanku chakra, hastham, padam, arch, base or other alankaram accessory.

Fit depends on deity, idol size, posture, draping, hairstyle, face/head width, ornament type and the product's actual measured dimensions.

## Measurements To Note

| Measurement | What to write |
|---|---|
| Idol height | From base/feet to top of head or crown area |
| Head or face width | For crown, mukut, kireedam and face accessories |
| Crown space height | Available height above the head |
| Crown depth / circumference | Needed for full-round or deep crowns |
| Neck or upper chest width | For short necklace placement |
| Desired haram drop | From neck to where the haram should end |
| Waist placement width | For vaddanam / oddiyanam after dressing |
| Ear / nose / forehead placement | For earrings, nose ring, tilak or namam |
| Arch / base space | For prabhavali, peedam, lotus asana or altar accessories |

## Photos To Send For Fit Help

- Front photo of the full idol.
- Side photo of the idol.
- Close-up photo of the placement area.
- One photo with a measuring tape visible.
- Photo after dressing or draping if the jewellery will sit over fabric.

## Fit Caveats To Mention

- Seated idol.
- Trunk direction for Ganesha.
- Flute, peacock feather or hand position for Krishna.
- Hairstyle, existing crown, bun or hair crown.
- Dress volume, saree drape or shoulder width.
- Weapons, shanku chakra, namam, thiruman, tilak or other deity-specific symbols.
- Altar depth, background frame, arch or mandapam space.

General deity jewellery is not automatically universal. Use it only where size, placement, symbol, posture and draping match. If unsure, share photos and measurements with Golden Collections before ordering.
