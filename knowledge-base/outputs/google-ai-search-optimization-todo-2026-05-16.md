## Core Interpretation

Google's guidance says generative AI visibility in Google Search is still based on normal Search fundamentals: indexed pages, crawlable content, helpful and reliable pages, clear technical structure, ecommerce/local business data, and genuinely useful first-hand content.

For Golden Collections, do not treat AEO/GEO as a separate trick. Treat it as better SEO, better product data, stronger proof, and clearer buyer guidance.
