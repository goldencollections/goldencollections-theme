### Partner Resource Description

### What A Teacher Can Share With Families

- Start with the teacher's list.
- Confirm regular, real kemp, or black kemp direction before ordering.
- Check short necklace, long haram, nethi chutti, headset, mattal, earrings, vanki, vaddanam/oddiyanam, rakodi, jada, flowers, and ghungroo/salangai.
- Trial the jewellery with costume, hair, and makeup before the final week.
- For children and young teens, check weight, scale, pinning, and comfort carefully.
- For international orders, leave extra time after delivery for customs, local delivery, trial, and teacher approval.
