# Testimonial Request Templates: Temple, Devotee, And Custom Deity Buyers

Draft status: Authority-support templates for truthful review collection. Use only with real buyers and do not offer incentives in exchange for positive reviews.

Source facts used: Golden Collections serves temple committees, priests, devotees, and deity alankaram buyers; deity jewellery includes crowns/mukut/kireedam, harams, earrings, vaddanam, pendants, nose rings, tilak/namam, shanku chakra, hands and legs, Varalakshmi faces/idols, vagamalai/thomala, arches, banana trees, lotus, and stands; fit depends on deity, idol size, ornament type, posture, and measured dimensions.

## Template 1: Temple Committee / Priest

Subject: Could you share your Golden Collections alankaram experience?

Namaste [Name],

Thank you for choosing Golden Collections for [temple/deity name] alankaram. We would be grateful if you could share a short testimonial about the jewellery or accessories you ordered.

Helpful details to include:

- Deity name and ornament type, such as crown/mukut/kireedam, haram, vaddanam, earrings, namam/thiruman, shanku chakra, arch, or other alankaram item.
- Whether the sizing and fit guidance helped.
- Your experience with finishing, stones, plating appearance, packing, and delivery.
- Any festival or pooja context, if you are comfortable sharing it.

You can reply in 3-5 sentences, or send a short video/photo with your permission to use it on Golden Collections pages and social posts.

Warm regards,  
Golden Collections
