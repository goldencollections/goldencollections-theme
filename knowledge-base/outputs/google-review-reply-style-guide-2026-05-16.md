# Golden Collections Google Review Reply Style Guide

Date: 2026-05-16  
Status: draft for owner approval

## Tone

Reply as Golden Collections, not as a generic ecommerce store. Keep the tone warm, specific, calm and useful. Mention the product, use case, occasion, delivery country/city, or fit detail when the reviewer gave one. Do not overdo keywords, do not sound automated, and do not make unsupported claims about certificates, perfect fit, temple approval, or guaranteed delivery timelines.

## Positive Reviews

Use three moves: thank the customer, acknowledge the exact use case, and add one useful context line for future buyers. Good details to reflect back include Varalakshmi crown or face size, deity/idol height, Bharatanatyam or Kuchipudi performance, arangetram planning, real kemp, international delivery, packing, video call support, or WhatsApp fit guidance. A natural closing can invite them to return for the next performance, pooja, festival, or alankaram need.

Example:

Thank you, [Name]. We are happy the [product/use case] worked well for your [idol size / dance performance / occasion]. For customers choosing a similar item, checking [fit detail] before ordering is always helpful. We would be glad to help again for your next alankaram, pooja, performance or festival preparation.

## Negative Or Mixed Reviews

Acknowledge the specific issue without arguing, blaming the courier, or debating the customer publicly. Move the resolution to WhatsApp, phone or email and ask for the order number/photos only if needed. Keep the public reply short; the goal is to show future buyers that Golden Collections takes fit, packing, delivery, and support issues seriously.

Example:

Thank you for letting us know, [Name]. We are sorry the [fit/delivery/packing/product] experience did not meet expectations. Please message us on WhatsApp at +91 7337294499 or email support@goldencollections.com with your order details, and our team will review this with you directly.

## Never Say

- Do not ask the customer to change or remove a negative review.
- Do not offer discounts, gifts, refunds, or benefits in exchange for a review.
- Do not say temple-approved, priest-approved, certified, official supplier, guaranteed fit, or guaranteed delivery unless separately confirmed and safe.
- Do not reveal private order, address, phone, temple, family, or event details.
- Do not paste the same reply under every review.

## Review Themes To Capture

During monthly or quarterly review audits, tag recurring review language into content ideas:

- Deity/idol fit: deity name, idol size, crown/haram/vaddanam/earring fit.
- Dance use: Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, arangetram, kids sets, performance deadline.
- Trust: video call, WhatsApp support, packing, finishing, stone work, plating appearance.
- Shipping: India delivery, Hyderabad local delivery, US/UK/Australia/Malaysia/Singapore/Europe delivery.
- Seasonal: Varalakshmi, temple alankaram, festival preparation, pooja decor.
