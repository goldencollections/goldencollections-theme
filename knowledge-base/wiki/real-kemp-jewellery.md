# Real Kemp Jewellery

Backlinks: [[business-entity.md]], [[search-entity-map.md]], [[content-roadmap.md]]

## Definition

Real kemp jewellery at Golden Collections is premium South Indian temple/dance jewellery made for Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, bridal, and traditional festive use. It typically uses antique gold-plated brass or copper bases, handcrafted temple motifs, and red, green, or white Kemp/Kempu stones.

## Customer-Facing Stone Language

Use simple customer language: `Kemp stones` or `Kempu stones`.

Avoid technical gem-setting details unless the owner specifically requests an education page for advanced buyers.

## Price Position

Real kemp is a premium range. A full set usually costs INR 30,000 to INR 2 lakh, depending on components, workmanship, stone setting, and finish.

## Difference From Regular Bharatanatyam Jewellery

Regular Bharatanatyam/Kuchipudi full sets usually sell from INR 2,700 to INR 10,000. Golden Collections positions this range as quality-focused regular dance jewellery with better finishing and longer-use durability than cheaper similar designs.

Real kemp jewellery is the higher-priced traditional premium range, with brass/copper base, 24 carat high gold plating, Kemp/Kempu stones, and more detailed handcrafted temple work.
