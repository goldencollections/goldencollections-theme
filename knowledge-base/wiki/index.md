### [[real-kemp-jewellery.md]]

Owner-confirmed real kemp definition, positioning, materials, pricing, and search synonyms.

Key facts:

- Real kemp is a premium South Indian temple/dance jewellery range.
- Use customer-facing language: `Kemp stones` or `Kempu stones`.
- Full sets usually cost INR 30,000 to INR 2 lakh.
