## Minimum Photo Set

Capture these in clear phone photos with good light:

1. Anil measuring total idol height.
2. Anil measuring head or face width for crown/kireedam fit.
3. Crown/kireedam placed near or on a suitable idol, with face visibility clear.
4. Short necklace or haram placed on an idol, showing chest/neck scale.
5. Optional vaddanam or waist ornament placement, if a suitable idol is available.
6. Optional hastham/padam placement for Varalakshmi/Lakshmi/Amman context.

## Optional Short Video

Record one 30-60 second vertical or horizontal phone video.

Suggested talking points:

- measure idol height first
- measure head or face width before choosing a crown
- check chest width and necklace length for haram fit
- share photos before ordering if unsure
- fit depends on idol posture, crown shape, drape, and placement

Do not make universal fit claims or ritual/procedure instructions.

## Safe Caption Angles

- "Anil checking idol height before recommending deity jewellery size."
- "Crown fit depends on head width, crown shape, and face visibility."
- "Haram length should match the idol's chest area and drape space."
- "For Varalakshmi, fit can depend on face width, crown space, saree drape, and setup style."

## Avoid

- Do not say every ornament fits every idol.
- Do not claim official temple approval or priest approval.
- Do not mention exact material/plating unless verified for the specific product.
- Do not show private customer/order information.
- Do not include customer or temple identity unless permission and wording are already confirmed.
