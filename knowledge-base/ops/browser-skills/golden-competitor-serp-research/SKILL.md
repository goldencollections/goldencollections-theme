# Golden Competitor SERP Research

Use this playbook to research search results and competitors without creating AI slop.

## Safety And Ethics

- Do not copy competitor copy, images, structure, or proprietary claims.
- Do not scrape at scale.
- Do not create pages just because a keyword exists.
- Do not treat generic blog rankings as proof of buyer intent.

## Before Browser

Read:

- `knowledge-base/ops/golden-collections-program.md`

## Research Workflow

1. Define the buyer question or money page first.
2. Search in an incognito/private browser where practical.
3. Record the query, location/language assumptions, and date.
4. Review top organic results, shopping/module results, image/video results, and AI/answer result if visible.
5. Classify intent:
   - product/collection
   - local/store
   - informational
   - comparison
   - how-to/fit
   - ritual/cultural
6. Identify what winning pages prove:
   - product availability
   - size/fit clarity
   - real photos/video
   - reviews
   - price/shipping clarity
   - founder/expertise
   - structured data
   - internal links
7. Compare Golden Collections against those proof signals.

## Golden Collections Filter

Prioritize:

- deity jewellery fit and measurement confidence
- collection/product page improvements
- proof blocks and real images/video
- FAQs based on actual customer confusion
- internal links to existing money pages
- Merchant/search snippets and schema alignment

Avoid:

- bulk AI blog generation
- generic "what is" articles when buyer wants a product page
- unsupported cultural/religious claims
- creating new pages with no proof, products, or conversion path

## Output

Report:

- Query researched
- Search intent
- What Google/AI seems to reward
- Golden Collections gap
- Recommended page/action
- Evidence links
- Whether to act now or wait

One useful product/collection/proof improvement is better than many thin articles.
