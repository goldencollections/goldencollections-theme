# 01 Research

Use this step after topic intake.

The goal is to gather enough evidence to choose the correct content angle before drafting.

## Required Reads

Always read:

- `blog-system/knowledge-snapshot.md`
- `knowledge-base/wiki/business-entity.md`
- `knowledge-base/wiki/search-entity-map.md`
- `knowledge-base/wiki/content-roadmap.md`
- `knowledge-base/wiki/open-questions.md`

Read as needed:

- `knowledge-base/wiki/real-kemp-jewellery.md`
- `knowledge-base/wiki/deity-compatibility-model.md`
- `knowledge-base/wiki/shopify-custom-data-model.md`

## Live Research

Use live web research when creating current SEO guidance, competitor analysis, or 2026 AI-search recommendations.

Research:

- buyer questions
- competitor coverage
- missing angles in competitor content
- regional name variants that appear in real search usage
- current search features or AI overview style answers
- Shopify collection/product pages on Golden Collections when URLs are available

Prefer primary or reliable sources for factual claims. Do not copy competitor language.

## GSC Check

If a GSC export is available in `knowledge-snapshot.md` or provided by the owner:

- identify queries with impressions and positions 11-20
- map query to current URL
- determine if the query deserves a new page, content refresh, FAQ addition, or internal-link improvement
- mark terms that should appear in title, H1, intro, FAQ, or anchor text

If no GSC export is available, write: `GSC data not available for this run`.

## Quality Bar

The research is complete when it can answer:

- who is the buyer?
- what decision are they trying to make?
- which Golden Collections collection or product family should this support?
- which regional terms matter?
- what facts are confirmed?
- what must be flagged before drafting?
