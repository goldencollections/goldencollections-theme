# 04 Draft

Use this step after the content brief is approved or accepted for drafting.

## Voice

Golden Collections voice is:

- specialist
- warm
- culturally grounded
- practical for buyers
- clear about range, fit, and use
- not generic fashion copy

Write as a knowledgeable store helping a real customer choose correctly.

## Opening Rule

Open with the buyer's real situation or question, not a dictionary definition.

Good:

`If you are preparing for an arangetram, real kemp jewellery is usually considered when the family wants a traditional, premium stage look that can be kept beyond one performance.`

Weak:

`Kemp jewellery is a type of jewellery.`

## Fact Rules

- Every factual claim must trace to the wiki, owner-confirmed facts, Shopify data, live source, or cited research.
- Do not publish uncertain claims.
- Mark uncertain claims as `Needs confirmation`.
- Do not use certificate language.
- Do not make unsupported plating thickness claims for regular Bharatanatyam or Kuchipudi jewellery.
- For real kemp, say `Kemp stones` or `Kempu stones`.
- Avoid technical stone names unless the owner asks for a specialist education page.
- Do not describe Golden Collections' own regular Bharatanatyam/Kuchipudi range as `imitation kemp`. Use `regular Bharatanatyam jewellery`, `regular Kuchipudi jewellery`, `regular dance jewellery`, or `regular kemp-style dance jewellery` depending on context.

## Required Content Elements

Every draft should include:

- clear H1
- useful introduction
- sectioned body with buyer-focused headings
- natural regional vocabulary
- at least one relevant Golden Collections collection or product reference
- size or fit guidance when relevant
- care or storage guidance before the FAQ when relevant
- selection guidance when relevant
- internal links
- FAQ block
- clear next action

## EEAT Signals

Use specific facts where relevant:

- Golden Collections started in 2012.
- Founder and public leader: Anil Tunk.
- Location: Secunderabad/Hyderabad, Telangana, India.
- Specialist catalog: Bharatanatyam jewellery, Kuchipudi jewellery, deity jewellery, kemp temple jewellery, and Hindu idol alankaram.
- Partner artisans make regular, premium kemp, and deity jewellery.
- Safe claims: handcrafted, hand-finished, handmade, artisan-made, temple craft, Hyderabad atelier.
- Quality checks include stone quality, gold plating, finishing, joints, packing, and shipping checks.
- Active shipping regions include India, US, UK, Australia, Malaysia, Singapore, and Europe.

Do not force all trust facts into every draft. Use the facts that help the buyer decision.

## Regional Vocabulary Rule

Use regional terms naturally in sentences, not as keyword-stuffed lists.

Good:

`Many dancers search for the waist belt as oddiyanam in Tamil contexts, vaddanam in Telugu contexts, or kamarband in broader Indian search.`

Weak:

`Oddiyanam vaddanam kamarband kati sutra waist belt buy online.`

## Closing Rule

Close with one clear next action:

- shop the relevant collection
- compare two ranges
- ask for WhatsApp fit help
- read a related size guide
- choose a seasonal checklist
