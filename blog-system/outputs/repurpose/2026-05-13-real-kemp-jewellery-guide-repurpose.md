## Quora Or Reddit-Style Answer

Question: What is real kemp jewellery?

Real kemp jewellery is usually understood as a premium South Indian temple and dance jewellery range used for Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, arangetram, bridal classical styling and traditional occasions.

At Golden Collections, real kemp means jewellery with antique gold-plated brass or copper bases, handcrafted temple motifs and red, green or white Kemp/Kempu stones. It is different from regular Bharatanatyam or Kuchipudi dance jewellery, which is usually a lower-priced alloy-metal range.

The difference matters because real kemp is generally more expensive, noticeably heavier, and chosen for major performances or long-term use. Before buying, check the purpose, component list, matching finish, dancer comfort, delivery timing and care instructions.
