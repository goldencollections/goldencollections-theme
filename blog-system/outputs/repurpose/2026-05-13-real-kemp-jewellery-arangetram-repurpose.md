## Quora Or Reddit-Style Answer

Question: How do I choose real kemp jewellery for an arangetram?

Start with the teacher's required ornament list, not with the design alone. For arangetram, the jewellery has to work with the costume, dancer size, hair setting, and the full stage look.

The pieces to check usually include short necklace, long haram, earrings, mattal or ear chains, nethi chutti, vaddanam or oddiyanam, vanki, rakodi, and jada accessories, depending on the teacher and dance style.

Real kemp is usually chosen when the family wants a premium South Indian temple jewellery look and the dancer may continue performing after arangetram. It is different from regular Bharatanatyam jewellery in material expectation, finish, workmanship, and budget.
