## Quora Or Reddit-Style Answer

Question: What items are needed for Varalakshmi alankaram?

It depends on the setup. A full Varalakshmi idol or doll needs a different checklist from an Amman mugham or kalasam setup.

For the visible alankaram, most families should check the face or idol, crown or kireedam, necklace or haram, hastham and padam, earrings, vaddanam or oddiyanam, and thomala or vagamalai where there is enough shoulder or drape space. Decor items such as banana tree, coconut stand, lotus asana, and arch depend on altar space and family tradition.
