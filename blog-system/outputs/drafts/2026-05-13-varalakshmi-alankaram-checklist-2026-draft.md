## Size Checks Before You Order

For deity jewellery, size is not just about idol height. Posture, face width, dress volume, hair style, and altar space can all affect the final look.

Before ordering, check:

- full idol or doll height
- face height and face width
- crown placement area
- neck or chest width
- waist area for vaddanam or oddiyanam
- shoulder width for thomala or bhujalu
- altar depth and background space
- whether the jewellery is tied, pinned, placed, or draped

For general/common deity accessories, use them only where the size and placement fit. A piece that works for one Lakshmi or Amman idol may not automatically fit another.

Most buyers should first check the face or idol, crown, necklace or haram, hastham and padam, earrings, nose ring, vaddanam or waist belt, and thomala or vagamalai where the setup has space. The exact list depends on whether you are decorating a full idol, doll, Amman face, or kalasam setup.

### Is the checklist different for a kalasam, Amman face, and full idol?

Yes. A kalasam or Amman mugham setup may focus more on the face, crown, flowers, saree drape, and surrounding decor. A full idol or doll can also need hastham, padam, waist belt, necklace placement, thomala, and base decor.

### How do I choose the right size for Varalakshmi jewellery?

Measure the idol or setup first. Check idol height, face width, crown placement, chest or neck width, waist area, and altar space. For items like crown, vaddanam, and thomala, visual scale matters as much as the listed size.

### Are banana tree decor, coconut stand, lotus asana, shanku chakra, and arch required?
