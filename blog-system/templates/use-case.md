# Template: Use Case Guide

## Purpose

Guide buyers shopping for a specific event, performance, ritual, or customer situation.

## Ideal Use Case

Use for arangetram, stage performances, dance school orders, temple festivals, Varalakshmi pooja, or bridal temple jewellery styling.

## Section Structure

1. Use case introduction
2. What the buyer needs to decide
3. Recommended product families
4. Size, fit, timing, or budget considerations
5. Regional terms
6. Related collections
7. FAQ
8. Next action

## Word Count Range

800-1,400 words.

## Schema Type

`Article` or `BlogPosting`; `FAQPage` when visible.

## Internal Link Requirements

Link to the most relevant product families and one related buying or size guide.

## FAQ Count

4-6 questions.

## EEAT Signals

Use real Golden Collections category facts, shipping facts, and quality-check facts where they help the use case.
