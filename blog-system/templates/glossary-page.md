# Template: Glossary Page

## Purpose

Create a durable definition and internal-linking hub for regional ornament names and related terms.

## Ideal Use Case

Use when many customers search different names for the same or related product family.

## Section Structure

1. Direct answer
2. Alternate names
3. Where the ornament is used
4. How to choose or size it
5. Related ornaments
6. Golden Collections collections to explore
7. FAQ

## Word Count Range

600-1,100 words.

## Schema Type

`Article` or `FAQPage` when visible.

## Internal Link Requirements

Link to every relevant collection and related glossary page.

## FAQ Count

3-5 questions.

## EEAT Signals

Use only controlled regional vocabulary from the wiki or owner-confirmed sources.
