# Template: History And Explainer

## Purpose

Build cultural context, answer-engine authority, and internal-linking value.

## Ideal Use Case

Use when buyers or search engines need a clear explanation of a tradition, ornament, term, or jewellery family.

## Section Structure

1. Buyer or cultural question introduction
2. Concise explanation
3. Historical or cultural context
4. How the item is used today
5. Regional names and related terms
6. Golden Collections product context
7. FAQ
8. Related guides and collections

## Word Count Range

900-1,600 words.

## Schema Type

`Article` or `BlogPosting`; use `FAQPage` only with visible FAQs.

## Internal Link Requirements

Link to glossary terms, collection pages, and practical buying guides.

## FAQ Count

4-6 questions.

## EEAT Signals

Be culturally careful. Mark ritual or historical claims as `Needs confirmation` unless sourced.

## Example Outline

Topic: What Is Kemp Jewellery?

- H1: What Is Kemp Jewellery? Meaning, Uses, and How It Is Worn
- Why buyers search for kemp and kempu jewellery
- How kemp is connected to South Indian temple jewellery styling
- How kemp is used in Bharatanatyam and Kuchipudi
- Real kemp vs regular kemp-style dance jewellery
- Regional and search terms
- FAQ
- Explore Golden Collections kemp jewellery
