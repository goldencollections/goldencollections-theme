# Template: Seasonal Guide

## Purpose

Capture time-sensitive demand with useful planning content.

## Ideal Use Case

Use for Varalakshmi, arangetram season, major festivals, wedding season, and seasonal product planning.

## Section Structure

1. Seasonal situation introduction
2. When to plan
3. What to buy or prepare
4. Size, fit, or compatibility checks
5. Shipping and stock timing
6. Regional vocabulary
7. FAQ
8. Seasonal next action

## Word Count Range

800-1,400 words.

## Schema Type

`Article` or `BlogPosting`; `FAQPage` when visible.

## Internal Link Requirements

Link to seasonal collection, checklist, related product families, and support/contact path.

## FAQ Count

4-6 questions.

## EEAT Signals

Use confirmed seasonal windows only. Varalakshmi is confirmed as May-August. Other festival dates should be checked for the year before publishing.

## Example Outline

Topic: Varalakshmi Jewellery Planning Guide

- H1: Varalakshmi Jewellery Planning Guide: What to Choose Before the Festival Season
- Why May-August is the planning window
- Jewellery and alankaram items to consider
- Size and fit checks for faces, idols, and dolls
- Regional and deity names
- Shipping timing for India and international buyers
- FAQ
- Shop Varalakshmi jewellery and decoration
