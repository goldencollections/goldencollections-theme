# Template: Size And Fit Guide

## Purpose

Reduce sizing confusion and help buyers choose confidently.

## Ideal Use Case

Use for deity jewellery, deity crowns, kids dance jewellery, waist belts, head ornaments, or any product where fit affects purchase confidence.

## Section Structure

1. Fit problem introduction
2. What measurements matter
3. How to measure
4. Size or age/idol-height guidance
5. Fit caveats
6. Product or collection references
7. FAQ
8. WhatsApp or support next action

## Word Count Range

800-1,400 words.

## Schema Type

`Article`, `BlogPosting`, or `FAQPage` when visible. Use `Product` only when product facts are verified.

## Internal Link Requirements

Link to relevant collection, support/contact path, and related compatibility guide.

## FAQ Count

5-7 questions.

## EEAT Signals

Use measured product facts only when available. If fit is inferred, mark it clearly or ask owner to confirm.
