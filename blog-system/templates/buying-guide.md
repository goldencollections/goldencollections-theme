# Template: Buying Guide

## Purpose

Help a buyer choose the right product, range, size, or collection.

## Ideal Use Case

Use when the topic has commercial intent and the buyer is deciding what to purchase.

## Section Structure

1. Buyer situation introduction
2. Who this guide is for
3. What to look for
4. Range or product options
5. Size, fit, or use guidance
6. Regional terms buyers may search
7. Recommended collections or products
8. Diaspora or delivery guidance where relevant
9. Golden Collections trust and quality notes
10. Care instructions, optional and placed before FAQ when relevant
11. FAQ
12. Next action

## Word Count Range

900-1,500 words.

## Schema Type

`Article` or `BlogPosting`; add `FAQPage` only when the FAQ is visible.

## Internal Link Requirements

Link to at least one collection, one related guide, and one glossary or support page if available.

## FAQ Count

4-6 questions.

## EEAT Signals

Use relevant Golden Collections facts: started in 2012, Secunderabad/Hyderabad, specialist catalog, partner artisans, quality checks, shipping regions.
