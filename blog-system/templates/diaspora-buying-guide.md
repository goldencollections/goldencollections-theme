# Template: Diaspora Buying Guide

## Purpose

Help international buyers trust the purchase, choose correctly, and understand shipping or sizing before ordering from India.

## Ideal Use Case

Use for US, UK, Australia, Malaysia, Singapore, Europe, and other diaspora customers buying Bharatanatyam, real kemp, deity jewellery, or Varalakshmi items online.

## Section Structure

1. International buyer situation introduction
2. What to check before ordering
3. Product or range explanation
4. Size and fit guidance
5. Shipping and timing expectations
6. Trust signals
7. Regional terms buyers may know from home
8. FAQ
9. Next action

## Word Count Range

900-1,500 words.

## Schema Type

`Article` or `BlogPosting`; `FAQPage` when visible.

## Internal Link Requirements

Link to relevant collection, size guide, contact/WhatsApp path, and related buying guide.

## FAQ Count

5-7 questions.

## EEAT Signals

Use confirmed international shipping regions and timelines. Avoid customs, duties, or return claims unless sourced from live policy pages.
