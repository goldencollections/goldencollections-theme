# Template: Collection Support Page

## Purpose

Support a Shopify collection with buyer guidance, FAQs, internal links, and conversion-focused content.

## Ideal Use Case

Use when the topic should help shoppers choose products inside an active collection.

## Section Structure

1. Collection-specific buyer introduction
2. What this collection includes
3. How to choose
4. Size, fit, material, or range notes
5. Regional terms
6. Related collections
7. FAQ
8. Shop next action

## Word Count Range

500-1,000 words for collection copy; 900-1,400 words for a supporting blog/page.

## Schema Type

`CollectionPage`, `ItemList`, and visible `FAQPage` where appropriate.

## Internal Link Requirements

Link to parent collection, child collections, related guides, and high-priority products when available.

## FAQ Count

4-6 questions.

## EEAT Signals

Use specific Golden Collections trust facts without crowding the shopping page.

## Example Outline

Topic: Kemp Jewellery Collection Support

- H1: Kemp Jewellery for Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, and Traditional Styling
- What buyers can find in the collection
- How to choose short necklace, long haram, headset, mattal, vanki, and waist belt
- Real kemp vs regular range note
- Regional terms: kemp, kempu, temple jewellery
- Related collections
- FAQ
- Shop the kemp jewellery collection
