## Blog Architecture And Menu Strategy

Last updated: 2026-05-13  
Source: Owner-confirmed strategy decision, Shopify menu check, `blog-system/06-shopify-package.md`

- Use one primary content hub for new buying guides, size guides, comparisons, checklists, glossary pages, and collection-support content: `Golden Collections Jewellery Guides`.
- Primary blog handle: `jewellery-guides`.
- Do not create separate thin blogs for Deity Jewellery, Bharatanatyam Jewellery, or Kemp Jewellery at this stage.
- Use topic tags inside `jewellery-guides` to build clusters without fragmenting authority:
  - `deity jewellery`
  - `Bharatanatyam jewellery`
  - `kemp jewellery`
  - `real kemp jewellery`
  - `buying guide`
  - `size guide`
  - `comparison`
  - `checklist`
  - `glossary`
- Shopify tag-filtered URLs may be used for navigation once a cluster has enough useful content, for example `/blogs/jewellery-guides/tagged/bharatanatyam-jewellery`.
- Do not add menu links to empty or near-empty tag pages. Add a tag-filtered page to the menu only after that cluster has at least 3 strong published articles.
- Treat Shopify tag pages as navigation hubs first. For SEO, they become stronger only when the theme/page has enough visible context, article depth, and internal links.
- Reconsider separate blogs only after a cluster has at least 8 to 10 strong articles and a clear reason to split:
  - Bharatanatyam Jewellery Guides: consider after 8 to 10 strong dance posts.
  - Deity Jewellery Guides: consider after 8 to 10 strong deity posts.
  - Kemp Jewellery Guides: consider only if Kemp has enough standalone buying, comparison, glossary, and care content to justify a separate hub.
