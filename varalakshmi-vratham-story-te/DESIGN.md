# Golden Collections Varalakshmi Short - Visual Identity

## Style Prompt
Create a warm, devotional luxury short for Golden Collections: practical enough for a checklist, rich enough for deity jewellery. The mood should feel like a careful home-pooja preparation, with product clarity, temple warmth, antique gold, maroon silk, jasmine white, and polished wood. The video must feel owner-led and trustworthy, not generic festive stock content.

## Colors
- Antique gold `#D6AE4C` for highlights, borders, and CTA emphasis.
- Deep maroon `#5B1720` for title panels and brand grounding.
- Temple cream `#FFF3D6` for primary text.
- Polished wood `#2A160C` for dark backgrounds.
- Jasmine white `#F8F2E5` for checklist cards and small accents.
- Lamp amber `#F3A23A` for soft glows and transition wipes.

## Typography
- Headlines: `Georgia`, bold, large, restrained letter spacing.
- Body and captions: `Trebuchet MS`, regular to bold, high contrast against dark overlays.
- Use short phrases only. This is a Shorts video; every text frame must be readable in under two seconds.

## Motion
- Gentle focus-pull and gold light-wipe transitions.
- Product images should move slowly with subtle scale or pan, never spin or bounce.
- Checklist items enter one by one with measured confidence.
- Keep all motion devotional, elegant, and calm.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not use neon colors, tech gradients, confetti, or loud festival animation.
- Do not overcrowd the frame with long paragraphs.
- Do not make religious claims beyond the approved blog content.
- Do not show shanku chakra as a normal Varalakshmi checklist item.
- Do not use generic jewellery words like exquisite or stunning.
