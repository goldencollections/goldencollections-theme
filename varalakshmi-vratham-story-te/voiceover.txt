Planning Varalakshmi Vratham 2026? First decide your setup: full idol, Amman mugham, kalasam, or altar display. Then check the visible alankaram pieces: face, crown or kireedam, necklace or haram, hastham and padam, earrings, vaddanam, and thomala or vagamalai. Decor items like banana trees, coconut stand, lotus asana, and arch depend on your altar space and family tradition. Do not leave sizing and matching pieces until the last week.
